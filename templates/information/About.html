What is Psitelly?

Most of the scientific research is funded by people who are told: "You cannot understand it because it is complex!"
We developed a website that hopes to bring back to people what is their right: "To know!" ...

How?
Psitelly is a website that you can explain a scientific article in a simple language that is understandable for the general public.

In other words, Psitelly is basically a video website like YouTube but specialized for scientific articles. Let’s say you want to further understand the “Methods” section of a scientific article, you go to Psitelly and search the article and you can find videos created by the author or other experts explaining the “Methods” section.

If you feel that you have understood a paper (or your own paper) that you can explain it to your grandma, you can share this knowledge with your community.

We don't want our future generation to cite a post from social media in their logical argument.
