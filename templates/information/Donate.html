What is Psitelly?

Psitelly is basically a video website like YouTube but specialized for scientific articles. Let’s say you want to further understand the “Methods” section of a scientific article, you go to Psitelly and search the article and you can find videos created by the author or other experts explaining the “Methods” section. ...
